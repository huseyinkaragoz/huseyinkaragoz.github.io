Privacy Policy for QuickSudoku

Last updated: June 3, 2024

1. Introduction

FunnIT ("we," "our," or "us") operates the QuickSudoku mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

2. Information We Collect

QuickSudoku is designed with privacy in mind. Our app:

  • Does NOT collect personal information - No names, emails, or phone numbers
  • Does NOT require registration - Play immediately without accounts
  • Does NOT track user behavior - No analytics or tracking
  • Does NOT contain ads - No third-party advertising networks
  • Works completely offline - No internet connection required

3. Local Data Storage

QuickSudoku stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Game progress and current puzzle state
  • Game settings and preferences
  • Game statistics (games played, completion times)

This data never leaves your device and is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app.

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

We explicitly do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics
  • Crash reports with personal data
  • Social media information
  • Payment information (app is free)

5. Third-Party Services

QuickSudoku does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks. The app operates entirely offline and independently.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service is safe for all ages, including children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no special procedures needed for children's privacy. Parents can safely allow their children to use QuickSudoku without privacy concerns.

7. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there are no data security risks. All game data remains locally on your device and is protected by your device's security measures.

8. Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal data to:

  • Access or download
  • Correct or update
  • Delete or remove
  • Transfer to other services

Simply uninstalling the app removes all local game data.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
